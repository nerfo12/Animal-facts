Cats

Top 5 intresting facts about Cats

1.Cats sleep for around 16-18 hours a day. You might notice that your feline friend spends most of the day snoozing and only comes out at night!


2.Cats are often lactose intolerant. This means they can’t drink milk.


3.There are many foods you shouldn’t feed your cat, including grapes, raisins, onions, garlic and chives. These foods can make your cat very ill.


4.A cat’s sense of smell is around 14 times stronger than a human’s.


5.A cat has more than 230 bones in its body – a human has 206.
